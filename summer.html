Mashrome Favorites ❤️

Top Pick

Soul Summit

Fort Greene Park · Sundays · 3–8 PM

The closest thing NYC has to a perfect Mashrome dance event: outdoor summer soul, disco, funk, boogie, deep cuts, community, and zero nightclub nonsense.

Why it fits: starts in the afternoon, no dress code, no bottle service, multigenerational Brooklyn crowd.
❤️❤️❤️
R&B Sing-Along

R&B ONLY

Warsaw + various NYC venues · several times per year

A giant R&B sing-along built around Usher, Lauryn Hill, TLC, Mary J. Blige, D'Angelo, Destiny's Child, Alicia Keys, and more.

Why it fits: less “club night,” more collective nostalgia and dancing to songs everyone knows.
❤️❤️
Reliable Venue

Brooklyn Bowl

Williamsburg · often 7–10 PM

One of the easiest places in NYC to catch danceable live music without committing to a full nightclub evening.

Watch for funk, soul, Afrobeat, groove-heavy rock, roots, and DJ nights.
❤️❤️
Early Weeknight

Bryant Park Dance Parties

Midtown · summer season · usually early evening

Free outdoor dancing with beginner lessons before social dancing begins. Surprisingly elegant and low-pressure.

Why it fits: dance on a weeknight and still get a full night's sleep.
